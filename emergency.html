Emergency Care

Fast steps to keep your pet safe while you get help.

What to do right now

  1. 1
    Stay calm and secure your pet
    Move them away from danger and keep them still.
  2. 2
    Check breathing and bleeding
    Apply gentle pressure with a clean cloth if bleeding.
  3. 3
    Do not give them human medicine
    Many human medications are dangerous for pets.
  4. 4
    Call for guidance before you travel
    We can tell you the safest next steps based on symptoms.
  5. 5
    Travel safely (carrier or blanket)
    Keep them warm, secure, and as still as possible.
If your pet is unconscious, having seizures, or bleeding heavily: seek emergency help immediately.
