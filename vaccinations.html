Dog vaccination schedule

A simple guide to puppy and adult vaccines.

Age Vaccine Why
6-8 weeks First puppy vaccine Helps protect against common illnesses
10-12 weeks Second puppy vaccine Builds stronger protection
14-16 weeks Final puppy vaccine + Rabies Completes the puppy course
1 year Booster vaccines Keeps protection active
Adult Yearly boosters Recommended once a year

Safety & Care

  • Your pet may be tired for a day after vaccination
  • Contact us if you see swelling or unusual behavior.
  • Let your pet rest for the rest of the day.

Quick FAQs

Do I need to book?
Yes, please book so we can prepare for your pet.
Can my pet come if they feel sick?
Let us know first - we may ask you to come another day.
What should I bring?
